Privacy Policy & Legal

1. Information Collection

Fun N Food Family Restaurant Resort & Water Park (managed by SKCPL Group) collects personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and booking details when you make a reservation, submit an enquiry, or interact with our website. This information is strictly used to fulfill your requests, manage your bookings, and provide customer support.

2. Use of Information

We use your data to process bookings for our resort rooms, banquet halls, and water park tickets. We may also use your contact details to send important updates regarding your stay or promotional offers. Your data is kept secure and is never sold to third-party organizations.

3. Terms & Conditions

All guests are required to carry a valid government-issued ID upon check-in. The management reserves the right of admission to the water park and resort premises. Guests are expected to follow safety rules and guidelines posted around the swimming pool and rain dance areas. SKCPL Group is not responsible for the loss of any personal belongings.

4. Cancellation & Refund Policy

Room and venue bookings can be canceled up to 48 hours prior to the check-in date for a full refund (minus processing fees). Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in may be subject to a one-night cancellation charge. Booking advances for marriage gardens and banquets are non-refundable unless specified otherwise in your agreement.
