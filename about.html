About Fun N Food

Welcome to Fun N Food Family Restaurant Resort & Water Park, proudly part of the prestigious SKCPL Group. Located near BSNL Exchange, Tejaji Nagar on Khandwa Road, we are Indore's premier destination for leisure, celebrations, and corporate events.

As a proud member of the SKCPL Group, we bring over a decade of hospitality excellence to every guest. Whether you're looking for a thrilling water park experience, a luxurious venue for your dream wedding, or an exciting family day out — Fun N Food has it all.

We offer Indian families fun-filled and affordable vacations. Our property features a pristine swimming pool, high-energy rain dance area, massive cricket and football grounds, a badminton court, indoor games (snooker, billiards, carom, chess), a sprawling marriage garden, and a multi-cuisine restaurant. Experience the best of hospitality and entertainment under one roof!
